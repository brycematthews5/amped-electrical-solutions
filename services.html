What We Do

Electrical Services

Straightforward electrical work for homes, ranches, farms, and businesses across all of Platte County.

Residential Wiring & Repair

Whether it's a flickering light, a dead outlet, or a panel that's ready for an upgrade, we handle the everyday electrical issues that come up around the house.

  • Outlet & switch repair or replacement
  • Circuit breaker & panel upgrades
  • Indoor & outdoor lighting installation
  • Ceiling fan & ventilation wiring
  • Whole-home electrical inspections
  • Barn, shop & outbuilding wiring
  • Grain bin & irrigation electrical systems
  • Storefront & office electrical work
  • Equipment & motor hookups
  • Electrical maintenance for commercial properties

Commercial & Agricultural Electrical

Ranch and farm buildings have different electrical demands than a typical house — we know what it takes to wire them safely and keep them running.

Emergency Electrical Service

Some electrical problems can't wait for regular business hours. We offer 24/7 emergency service for issues that put your home, family, or livestock at risk.

Emergency Line
  • Power outages & tripped main breakers
  • Sparking outlets or burning smells
  • Repair broken underground lines
  • Well pump & livestock system failures
  • Full electrical wiring for new builds
  • Rewiring for remodels & additions
  • Panel & service upgrades for renovations
  • Coordination with builders & contractors

New Construction & Remodels

Building new or renovating an existing space? We'll wire it right from the start, working alongside your builder or contractor.

Not Sure Which Service You Need?
